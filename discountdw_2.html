Buy Online and Save Big

Discount Door and Window (DDW) is dedicated to providing the lowest cost retrofit doors and replacement vinyl windows in the San Diego area and beyond. We accomplish this by working with well established wholesalers, keeping overhead costs down to a minimum, and passing on the savings to you. More about Discount Door and Window

 

Good Quality Products We Believe In

The doors and windows we offer have been selected because of their modern engineering, durability, and energy efficient. The vinyl replacement windows and vinyl patio doors have a lifetime manufactures warranty, and depending on the door style we offer between 10 to 30 year warranties. The energy efficiency of these products will save you money on heating and cooling bills for years to come. We offer quality door installation and window installation with customer satisfaction guaranteed. Discount Door and Window offers these premium quality products at a cost below the suggested retail price.

 

San Diego Location & Shipping Information

Discount Door and Window is located in Scripps Ranch in San Diego, California 10095 Scripps Ranch Court Ste. B San Diego, CA 92131 . Customers in La Jolla, Carlsbad, Poway, San Marcos, El Cajon, Ramona and surrounding San Diego County can pickup doors and windows at our location to save on shipping charges. It usually takes between 2 or 3 weeks to have your order ready to be picked up. Customers outside of San Diego can pay a nominal shipping fee to have the products delivered to your location. Since the weight, size, and distance the products needs to travel varies, we use a quote shipping system to determine the lowest cost solution. This means your shipping cost is calculated after you place your order. You are not committed to purchasing your products until you approve the shipping quote we provide within 48 hours.
